FileWeave: A social cloud storage solution.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

FileWeave is a single cloud storage platform that keeps your personal, school, and professional files organized in one place. Staying connected with other users, FileWeave helps you stay connected through being able to easily collaborate and download editable files to enhance your workflow.

PROBLEM

Ever have issues keeping your cloud storage organized? Between work, family, and your personal files, it’s easy to get disorganized. When you’re working on multiple platforms to store and share personal and professional life documents, streamlined workflow is inhibited.

SOLUTION

FileWeave as a minimum viable product provides file storage, creation, and collaboration. With enhanced organizational features, and the capability to easily share and display your files for all realms of your life, FileWeave is the answer to your cloud storage needs.

RESEARCH OVERVIEW

First off, I had to test the client's, and my own assumptions about cloud storage users needed to be confirmed or denied with research. Identifying use context and needs of potential users was done through deploying a user survey. With the initial survey responses, an empathetic understanding of how individuals use and think about cloud storage was gained.

  • Survey Findings
  • 40% of users are happy using multiple cloud storage platforms for different tasks.
  • 80% of users would benefit from expanding social network cloud features.
  • 87% of users see benefit of expanding collaboratve features.
  • 70% are very concerned with keeping files organized.
  • 85% say saving, viewing and editing files, are the most important functions.
  • 32% of users are unhappy with syncing across devices.

PERSONAS

After the survey results were analyzed, I created user personas to key in on user needs and frustrations as I designed FileWeave.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

I did a SWOT analysis to learn more about the ecosystem of cloud storage platfoms FileWeave was entering. I researched the ease of document uploading, navigation, organization, effectiveness of UI and UX, and task facilitation and special features, and yielded these results:

Positioning: Google drive positions itself as a file storage and syncing system, leveraging it's loyal user base. It is seen as a comprehensive storage and collaboration tools, as well as a key component of G Suite, to appeal to businesses.

Positioning: Dropbox positions itself as a backup and file sharing platform, centered around team collaboration and document storage. Dropbox is built for productivity as opposed to simply storage. Primary Audience: Dropbox is used mainly for B2B transactions, but also for individual use.

Positioning: Tresorit positions itself to be providing secure storage better than other cloud providers. Positions itself as a backup cloud storage site.

APPLYING THE RESULTS

  1. All three platforms are tapered to a specific type of storage, but users have to use different platforms based on their user purpose. FileWeave can provide one centralized platform.

  2. Dropbox lacks file creation, Tresorit lacks organization. FileWeave will allow you to store, edit and organize files.

  3. GoogleDrive lacks access to public social documents.FileWeave will have a social feed where you can download public documents, which will be what it’s known for.
